Episode 5: Building a Startup in Boise

Released July 1, 2025

In this episode, Boise‑based entrepreneur Sarah Hughes joins us to discuss how she transformed a hackathon idea into a thriving SaaS company. We dive into her journey from concept to funding, the local mentors who supported her and how Boise’s ecosystem nurtured her growth.

Show Notes

  • An overview of Sarah’s background and what inspired her idea
  • How she validated the concept at a local hackathon
  • Resources and mentors she leveraged in Boise
  • Lessons learned from fundraising and early customer acquisition
  • Advice for aspiring founders in the Treasure Valley
