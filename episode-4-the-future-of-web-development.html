Episode 4: The Future of Web Development

Released June 10, 2025

In Episode 4 we chat with two Boise developers about the rapid evolution of web technology. From WebAssembly enabling high‑performance browser apps to Rust replacing JavaScript in some tooling, we discuss the shifts shaping the future of web development and how AI is streamlining workflows.

Show Notes

  • An introduction to WebAssembly and why it matters
  • Rust’s growing role in front‑end tooling and back‑end services
  • How AI tools are improving code quality and developer productivity
  • What to learn next to stay ahead of the curve
