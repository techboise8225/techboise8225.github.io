About Boise Tech Connect

Boise Tech Connect started as a passion project to unite the local tech community. Our mission is to share knowledge, foster collaboration and highlight the people driving innovation in the Treasure Valley.

We operate as a lean side‑gig today, publishing a couple of posts and podcast episodes each month while attending community events. As support grows, we plan to transition to full‑time operations with more frequent content and larger events.
