Episode 3: Bootstrapping vs Venture Capital

Released May 5, 2025

In Episode 3 we sit down with serial entrepreneur Mike Palmer to discuss funding strategies for startups. He shares his experiences bootstrapping his first company and contrasts them with raising venture capital for his second. We cover the trade‑offs between ownership, growth velocity and risk.

Show Notes

  • What it means to bootstrap a startup and why some choose it
  • Pros and cons of seeking venture funding
  • Mike’s advice on deciding which path is right for you
  • The importance of aligning funding with personal and company goals
